Pho Recipe

While you’ll need to man handle a considerable mound of bones and beef, I think you might be surprised how straightforward it actually is to make pho. It’s more time than anything – and a very big pot!

Ingredients

  • Ginger
  • Onion
  • Cinnamon
  • Cardamom
  • Coriander
  • Star Anise
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Fish Sauce

Steps

  1. Remove impurities freom beef with a 5 minute boil, it's the path to a beautiful clear soup
  2. Be amazed at all the icky stuff that comes out
  3. Wash the bones to get all the icky scum off
  4. Simmer for 3 hours
  5. Remove brisket
  6. Simmer 40 minutes further
  7. Strain
